Thu Oct 15 / 8:30 – 9:00 / public

Welcome to the 2020 Online Conference of the Universities Art Association of Canada.

Although we are gathering virtually from near and far, we would nevertheless like to acknowledge that we, and our host institutions SFU and UBC, are located on the traditional, ancestral and unceded Salish territories of the xʷməθkwəy̓əm (Musqueam), Skwxwú7mesh (Squamish), and Səl̓ílwətaɬ (Tsleil-Waututh) Nations. We encourage you to take a moment to think about the land from which you are tuning in, as well.

We hope you’ll join us to celebrate the opening of #UAAC2020!
